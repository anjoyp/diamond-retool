About us

Diamond ReTool was born from the experience gained within a company that helped write the history of diamond tools and rollers in Turin, Italy.

The skills acquired over time, combined with the teachings handed down by those who have perfected the art of cutting individual diamonds, today represent a technical and human heritage of great value.

Studies, research, development, and specific techniques, honed over years of field experience, have given rise to a new opportunity: to offer a highly specialized service capable of responding concretely to the needs of modern industry.

Current context

New technologies and an unprecedented global crisis are putting manufacturing companies to the test.

Businesses increasingly find themselves caught between rising costs and a shortage of qualified personnel. Optimizing resources and reducing waste is no longer an option, but a strategic necessity.

Our vision

“A diamond is forever.”

It is precisely from this principle that Diamond ReTool's vision is born. If a diamond is forever, then the tools that employ it can continue to generate value well beyond their apparent life cycle.

Technology and experience:
a fundamental balance

Production processes evolve, but those who know the diamond understand that working such a unique material requires technical sensitivity.

Sight, hearing, and technical sensitivity are tools that still play a decisive role in final precision today.
